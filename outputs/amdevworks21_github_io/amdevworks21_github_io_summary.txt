SoutulKhair is a website for the Jamia Abul Khair Foundation, an organization dedicated to promoting Islamic education and spiritual guidance internationally. The site offers various resources including a book collection, online Quran courses (like Khair ul Aloom), and information about spiritual events and figures associated with the organization. Users can register for courses and explore biographies, audio galleries, and upcoming events. The foundation also focuses on providing facilities like study areas and personalized assistance for research.
