SoutulKhair is a website dedicated to providing Islamic education and spiritual guidance. They offer various Quranic courses, including Tajweed, Tafsir, and Nazrah, taught by experienced teachers. The site also features biographies of spiritual figures, an audio gallery, a book display, and information about upcoming Islamic events.  Furthermore, SoutulKhair promotes a library with extensive resources and encourages contributions to their educational foundation. Finally, the website offers registration for online Quran academy courses.
