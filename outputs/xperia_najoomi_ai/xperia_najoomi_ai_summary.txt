NAJMI XPERIA, powered by Najoomi Technologies, provides call centers with AI-driven solutions to analyze multilingual call recordings and agent performance. Their technology leverages Generative AI, speech emotion recognition, and natural language processing to improve quality assurance. NAJMI claims their services can save call centers up to 75% on quality assurance costs. They offer on-premises, outsourcing, and SaaS deployment models. The company is based in Delaware, United States.
