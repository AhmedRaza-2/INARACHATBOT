Inara Technologies is a solutions and services company specializing in digital transformation, particularly for service providers and media delivery. They offer expertise in areas like OSS/BSS, digital TV solutions, security, DevOps, analytics, and cloud orchestration. Inara also provides managed services and has experience in fiber optic network deployment.  They aim to optimize multi-vendor technology environments using a "Software First" strategy and DevOps culture. They are based in Islamabad, Pakistan.
