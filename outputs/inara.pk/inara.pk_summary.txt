Inara Technologies is a solutions and services company specializing in digital transformation and optimization for businesses in multi-vendor, multi-technology environments. They provide solutions for service providers, digital TV, security, DevOps, media delivery, analytics, cloud orchestration, and managed services.  Their services include fiber optic network deployment and project management. Inara leverages a "Software First" strategy and DevOps culture to deliver agile, reliable, and cost-effective solutions to help customers achieve operational excellence. The company is based in Islamabad, Pakistan.
